Call for Solvers

MaxSAT Evaluation 2024 invites submissions of MaxSAT solvers for evaluation.

Before Registering a Solver

Please read carefully the rules and details on the evaluation tracks and execution environment before proceeding with submitting your solver.

Note that solvers participating in the main track of the MSE2024 are expected to pass a regression suite that can be found at github. More details can be found on the rules page
